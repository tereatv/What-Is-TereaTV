01Overview

TereaTV is a streaming platform that delivers live television channels and on-demand movies and series over the internet. It runs across the United States, the United Kingdom, and Canada, with plans that start at $15 a month and a 24-hour trial for anyone who wants to test the service first.

Because several look-alike sites use a similar name, it is worth pointing out that the platform’s home is tereatv.net — that is where subscriptions, activation, and support are handled.

04Supported devices

The service works on Amazon Firestick and Fire TV, Android phones, tablets and TV boxes, Smart TVs from Samsung, LG and Android TV, iPhone and iPad, Windows, Mac, and MAG or Enigma2 boxes. Device-by-device steps are kept up to date on the setup guide.

05Try before you pay

If you’d rather test first, the platform offers a no-card 24-hour free trial. It opens the full channel lineup so you can check picture quality and stability on your own network before committing to a plan.
